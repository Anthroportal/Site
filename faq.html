FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Robin Hood

What's Anthroportal?

Anthroportal is a non-profit web portal that contains a list of films, events and communities and answers about Furry fandom.

Retsuko

What's the Furry Fandom?

The Furry Fandom is a community of people who share a passion for anthropomorphic animals.

Michiru Kagemori

How can I add my community?

To add your group read the community guidelines and submit a request through our site.

Mr. Wolf

What is a Meeting?

A meeting or community is a small group of people who periodically organize small events in the same city or region/conty.

Kristofferson

What is a Convention?

A convention is a recurring event organized by an organization where a group of people from all over the world meet.

Legoshi

What is an Anthro?

Anthro or anthropomorphic are animals with human characteristics or behaviors like the ancient Egyptian gods.

Nick Wilde

What is a Furry?

The term furry, that can be comparable to the term "Weeb" for fans of the Japanese culture, defines a person with a passion for anthropomorphic animals.

Porco Rosso

What is a Fursona?

A Fursona is an original fictional anthropomorphic character who represents the author and thus distinguishes him from other members of the community.

BoJack Horseman

What is a Cosplayer?

Cosplaying is the practice of wearing a costume that represents an existing popular character from a movie, anime, or video game.

The Baron

What is a Fursuiter?

Being a fursuiter is the practice of wearing a costume called "fursuit" representing an animal or its own original OC called fursona.

Fenneko

What's is a spotter?

The spotter is tasked with taking care of the fursuiter, giving him a drink and making sure he doesn't pass out from too much heat.

Diane Foxington

What is a furmaker?

A furmaker is a self-taught professional figure that dedicates theirself to the creation of bespoke suits called fursuit on commission.

Judy Hopps

What is a badge?

A badge is an identity card or more simply a drawing hung around your neck that represents your fursona containing social links or generic information about yourself.

Nazuna Hiwatashi

What do I need a badge for?

A badge is used to be recognized by friends at large conventions and it's useful when you meet a lot of new people.
