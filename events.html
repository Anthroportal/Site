The most important anthro conventions

Eurofurence

Eurofurence 26 will take place in summer 2022. Eurofurence is Europe's largest annual gathering of the European furry community.

Visit the site
Anthrocon

Anthrocon (abbreviated AC) is a furry convention taking place in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania each June or July.

Visit the site
Furizon

Furizon is the unique Italian furry convention for fun and adventure!

Visit the site
Confuzzled

ConFuzzled is the UK's largest furry convention, hosted at the Hilton Birmingham Metropole.

DenFur

DenFur is located in the capital of Colorado making it easy for locals and furs from around the world to be a part of our event and the Denver furry community.

Visit the site
Furpocalypse

Furpocalypse is a Halloween-themed Anthropomorphic convention in Stamford, Connecticut that raises money for local animal and social charities.

Visit the site
LondonFurs

LondonFurs is a large (and growing!) community of creative artists, costumers, and fans of anthropomorphised animals.

Visit the site
Weekend Atlanta

Furry Weekend Atlanta is a convention that takes place in downtown Atlanta, at the Marriott Marquis.
